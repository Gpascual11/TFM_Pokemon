SPEED
	- So unique and IMPORTANT
	- + HP -> + survival / + atk -> + damage...
	- SPEED determines the order in which pokemon move (fastest to slowest, ignoring priority)
	- +1 is the same as +100
	- important moves/items/abilities to upgrade speed
	
SPEED (GEN 8)
	- Before the speed was calculated on the start only.
	- Now it updates among the turn
		- Speed moves more powerful
	
PRIORITY
	- Some moves has higher or negative priority. Rest none.
	- A pkm using a move +1 will always move before a pkm using a move +0, regardless of speed stats
	- Ability prankster gives +1 priority to status moves.
	- When same in all (priority & speed) - 50% chance of each.
	

STATUS CONDITIONS - 1 at a time only
	- BURN, SLEEP, PARALYSIS, POISION (+ badly), FREEZE
	- BURN: 50% damage to physical attacks + 1/16 damage at the eot.
	- POISON: 1/8 damage/turn.
		- BADLY POISON: accumulative damage. 1/16 + 1/16 x turn. (Can reset ---------------------??--------------
	- PARALYSIS: 1/2 speed stat + 1/4 unable to move.
	- SLEEP: Unable to attack for 1-3 turns (1/3% each) - rest: sleep always 2 turns.
	- FREEZE: 1/5 per atk, unable to attack until thawed.
		Cannot freeze in sun, fire attack or scald thaws immediately.
		Can use certain attacks while frozen.
	- CONFUSION: 1/3 to fail atk and take damage based on your physical atk and def stats.

ABILITIES
	- Each pkm has 1 at a time, has to choose up to 3.
	- Can affect stats, def boost, offensive buffs, activate field conditions....
	
ITEMS
	- Each pkm can hold 1 item
	- Some dont work if held (potions, revives...)
	- Only used 30 at most in competitive.
	- Choice items- raise 1 stat 1.5x but only allow the first move until you switch.
	- Health restoring Berries, type reducing berries, offensive items...
	
NATURES
	- Multiplies one stat by 1.1x and another by 0.9x.
	- Sometimes is better to lower one stat...

EVs and IVS.
Effort Values and Individual Values
	- Both determine the actual stats of a pokemon in combination with Base Stats
	- IVs are set at birth, can be changed with hyper training. (lvl. 100 all IVs can be 31).
	- EVs at birth are 0. Are based on the training.

FIELD EFFECTS
	- Effects of whole or half of the board.
	- TRICK ROOM/TAILWIND/TERRAINS (1.3x/1.5x)/REFLECT,LIGHT SCREEN, AURORA VEIL/ 
	- Less common - MAGIC ROOM/WONDER ROOM/GRAVITY
